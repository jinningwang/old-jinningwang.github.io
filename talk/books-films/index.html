Stories of Your Life and Others – Ted Chiang

Louise Banks, a linguist, gains the ability to know the future by learning the heptapods’ language, a non-linear alien language. It means she still starts the love story with Ian Donnelly, although she has already known that her marriage with Ian Donnelly will break up because their daughter passed away accidentally.

Then there arises a question, will you still have the courage if you already know the ending, especially if it was a “bad” ending? I would say “yes” many years ago without hesitation because I believed experience is the most beautiful thing, even if there is no “happy” ending. However, now I think the courage to say “yes” is the hope to earn a workable solution even if it might need thousands of times of failure.However, now I think the courage to say “yes” is the hope to make a work one even it might needs thousand times of failure
